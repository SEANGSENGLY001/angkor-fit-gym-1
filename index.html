Common Questions

Quick answers before your first visit.

I am a beginner. Can I still join?

Yes. Most new members start as beginners and follow our guided starter path.

Do you have contracts?

No long lock-in. You can choose flexible monthly plans.

Do you have parking and showers?

Yes. On-site parking and clean shower rooms are available daily.

What language do coaches speak?

Khmer and English support is available across all core programs.

How long is a session?

Most sessions are 45 to 60 minutes depending on your program.

Can I try before membership?

Yes. Book a free trial session and meet a coach first.
